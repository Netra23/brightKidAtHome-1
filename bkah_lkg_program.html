LKG

Objective of our LKG program is to prepare children to perform age appropriate academic and extra- curricular activities meeting the requirements of various education board standards such as CBSE/ICSE aligned with NEP 2020. Children are engaged in various purposeful activities for the development of multiple intelligence. This group performs extensive Montessori activities. Montessori activities include not only refinement of Exercises of Practical Life but also perform various Montessori activities on Arithmetic, Language, Sensorial and Science. Interactive digital smart class activities make learning extremely enjoyable for the children. Children enjoy and have fun while interacting with teachers and peers. Parents get the academic support from our team and child friendly Apps and portal.


Key area's of development are:

  • Social and Emotional skills
  • Language Development
  • Reading & Writing
  • Phonics
  • Maths concepts
  • General Knowledge
  • Creativity and aesthetics
  • Talent Development
  • Life skills
